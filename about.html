Website


This website was made by me, Angad Sood, for my class on HTML and CSS.


In this website I try to showcase my skills by implementing the skills learned in the class on the aforementioned topics and build on top of them.


I see SpaceX implimenting my vision by making reusable rockets, landing them and flying them back again. I hope to be part of the future that helps my country and the world advance the spaceflight, thereby, giving a backup to the human civilization if an anomaly to the Earth everytime occurs.




Design


The design of this website is heavily inspired by the SpaceX website and a few other aerospace company websites. My motive was to add animations that instill a feeling of suspense and story to the otherwise empty space. This should especially be noticeable on the home page. I also wanted the website to be dynamic so I decided to use the underline plugin that changes its color everytime you revisit the website or the page and adds color to the page that still manages to keep up with its original objective- Space.




Creator



I come from Chandigarh, India. As a child, I was inspired by a show on Looney Tunes (an animated series) where in a distant future civilization, rockets were everywhere and using them was going to the top of your skyscraper away. Growing up I loved watching movies and reading about subjects related to Space.

In the US, I was fortunate to have worked with MIT's KitCube program focusing on replacing radio based communication with visible light based communication (less diffusion, less noise). We received a funding of $5M and finished 2nd in the NASA's CubeQuest project (best project gets to send its nano satellite to the moon) but the funding was not enough and the leads weren't able to cope up with the project and the academics at MIT.



But, having seen my country make the cheapest spacecraft to be sent to the Mars and other similar projects, I hope that I would be able to take part in and help be part of the generation that helps foster the next gen spacecrafts.
