It has been my lifelong dream to see humans reach another planet and colonize it.


The only way to make it to the other planets is to use rockets. Mars is our best bet in the near future. On the Info page I compare different rockets, propulsion methods, stages of a rocket and the vehicle of choice for humans to reach Mars.


Here's what Elon Musk thinks:


"You want to wake up in the morning and think the future is going to be great - and that's what being a spacefaring civilization is all about. It's about believing in the future and thinking that the future will be better than the past. And I can't think of anything more exciting than going out there and being among the stars."
